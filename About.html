About us!

Learn more about this website!

Studio Ghibli Food Recipes aims to teach and help people find their favorite studio ghibli food. In this website, you'll be able to find the recipes for each and every studio ghibli movie you watch, inculding Ponyo, Spirited Away, Kiki's Delivery Service, and etc. It's really suitable for people who aren't really experienced with cooking. We hope you find our recipes easy but delicous to make. Have fun making them!


About the Author:


The person who made this website is named Rhianna M. Arevalo. A grade 9 student from the section Emerald in OBLP. I am 14 years old and is a big fan of studio ghibli. I made this website because everytime a Studio Ghibli movie, I'd always find their food really good appetizing. I searched "Studio Ghibli Recipes" and nothing showed up. How could anyone not make a website of their food!! And that's how I got the idea of making this website for my ICT project.
