Kiki's Cake

Ingredients needed: chocolate cake mix, milk, sugar, icing
Steps:
1. Bake the chocolate cake mix and add milk andd sugar.
2. Melt chocolate and spread it all over the cake.
3. Draw a witch riding it's broom and add the name "Kiki" below.
4. Personalize your cake with unique decorations.

Spirited Away Cheesecake

Ingredients needed: cheesecake mix, water, milk, sugar, eggs, and yellow icing.
Steps:
1. Make the batter and bake the cake until brownish color.
2. Make the cheese icing.
3. Pour cheese icing on the cooled cake.

Marine's Strawberry Jammed Cookies

Ingredients needed: dough and strawberry jam/filling
Steps:
1. Make the dough by using a simple dough recipe.
2. Divide the cookie dough into equal balls and use a spoon to make a circle shape in the middle.
3. Bake the cookies for 30 mins.
4. Once baked, let the cookies cool down for 20-30 mins.
5. Fill the circle indent with strawberry jam or any jam you want to use.
