Beverage Recipes

Find and make our Studio Ghibli based recipes made for fans like you!


Coquette Hot Coco

Ingredients needed: choco mix, hot water, milk, and coquette mug.
Steps:
1. Find a mug that has ribbons/bows (can be diy).
2. Find any choco drink powder (ex: milo/ swismiss)
3. Mix both in the coquette mug.

Zesty Orange Juice

Ingredients needed: many oranges, water, tea cup, and sugar
Steps:
1. Squeeze a lot of oranges and take out the seeds.
2. Add a bit of cold water and sugar.

Green Tea

Ingredient needed:Tea mix, water, and teacup.
Steps:
1. Boil hot water and put it in the teacup.
2. Let the tea bag sit for whenever you want.
3. Take out the tea bag and drink while it's hot.
