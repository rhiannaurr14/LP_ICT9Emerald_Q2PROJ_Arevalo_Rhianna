Ponyo Ramen

Ingredients needed: Hard-boiled egg, ham, scallions, and instant ramen
Steps:
1. Hard-boil the eggs.
2. Boil and cook the instant ramen the ham.
3. Once the ramen and the eggs are cooked, place in one bowl.
4. Add green onion or scallions

Howl's Breakfast

Ingredients needed: fresh bacon and fresh eggs( for a better taste)
Steps:
1. Pre-heat the pan and pour the cooking oil.
2. Once hot enough, put the fresh bacon in the pan.
3. After, put the amount of eggs you need.
4. Take the bacon and eggs out of the pan and add some cheese (optional).

Japanese Cream Stew

Ingredient needed: Carrots, Flour, Potatoes, Onions, Chicken, Sprouts, Garlic, Salt and Pepper, Parsley
Steps:
1. Pan fry the potatoes, carrots, and onions.
2. Pan fry the diced chicken and sprouts (or broccoli).
3. Add 1 tbsp of butter and flour and cook out a roux.
4. Slowly add milk, and stir inbetween.
5. Add in reserved toppings.
6. Add water to loosen and add in a pinch of Garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
7. Lastly, Sprinkle a bit of parsley!
